Who We Are

We are a small team of scientists who have started to make headway into discovering new constellations and potential life on other planets and moons. Using our team of drones and rovers we've explored the surfaces of neighboring planets including Mars, Venus and Jupiter.

Currently we are working on sending a drone out to Neptune in hopes of bringing back samples from it's surface. We also believe that Pluto is a planet, even though it is smaller than some moons. Give Pluto it's planetory rights back! Please sign our petition on our form page.

New Topics

Our current project that we are working on is our expedition to send a rover to Neptune. After a successful expedition to Mars with our first rover we are looking forward to bringing samples back from Neptune. If you would like to support our upcoming projects feel free to donate to our Kickstarter campaign. Also in related news we are celebrating our first year anniversary of our trip to Mars. Come down to the company grounds and celebrate with us!

We have also partnered up with NASA and have been helping them with discovering new exoplanets. These exoplanets will become future expeditions in which we will bring back samples and study their importance and how they can be used to better our lives here on earth.
