Rover Expeditions

The Trip to Mars

The the trip to mars was a costly one but we mostly got our funding from company backers and by using online crowd-funding websites. With our rover being comparable to NASA's Curiosity expedition rover which is the size of a small SUV then it is expected that this is going to be a pricey journey. This expedition took months of planning and calculations to make sure everything was exact and perfect. We needed to be flawless in order for this to be a success and in order for us to be able to get funding for future expeditions. If this is seen as a failure then there goes our expedition program. Alas, everything went as planned and our rover (appropriately named Ares) got some great shots for us when it reached Mars.

Image: Picture of Mars from Ares the rover.

Rover Specs

As you can see these space exploration rovers are a little more than complicated. They are very intricate machines and take months to years to make. Ours took a little over a year to complete and get ready for its first trip, to Mars. Every rover has a body that houses all of its vital organs including its brain and energy source. The brain is nothing more than computer processors while the energy component is a battery. We also had temperature controls installed and a layer of insulation. To make the rover have a humans perspective a neck and head were installed with a camera attached at the top for a human-esque view. The rover is also outfitted with arms and small clamps for hands. Wheels are its source of mobility. Lastly it has antennas for its much needed communication aspect.

Image: Diagram of a rover.

Future Projects

Our current project is building an underwater rover to send to Neptune. We are currently in the beginning parts of planning but everything looks like its going well. Looking for new backers is tough but we a slowly getting to our goal. We should reach it within a few more months. Backers on our website who contribute will be able to come to our facilities and see first hand what the rover brings back from the watery giant. Find us on Kickstarter.
